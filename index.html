About Magic

Illusionist (magician) -
an artist demonstrating tricks based on certain physical and psychological phenomena

Personal qualities:
- Power
- Agility
- Intelligence
- Savvy

At the same time, the secret of the "miracles" seen by the audience must certainly remain unsolved

The most important task of the illusionist is to captivate the audience, and the focus to interest and give aesthetic pleasure
